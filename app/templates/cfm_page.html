About

CFM Repository

The faults of the CRESCENT Community Fault Model are three-dimensional and non-planar; however, for ease of use, the viewer offers a simplified two-dimensional map-based representation of the CFM. Click on each fault trace to access its description. Utilize the map controls to refine the display to your desired faults. Select the faults of interest by checking them, then use the View 3D button to examine the chosen faults in three dimensions. For further information and instructions on usage, please refer to the provided user guide.
